Hayot Dried Fruit

It is a major exporter of dried fruits, fruits, nuts, peanuts, oils and spices.

We have been on the market since 1999. We are confident that nothing compares to the taste of natural products. Therefore, we grow all our products ourselves without using any chemical additives.

We have an absolutely natural, environmentally friendly product. We carefully monitor quality: from the moment the seeds are planted and throughout the entire production process. We grow, select, sort, package and all this according to high international standards.

Particular attention to careful sorting. Only selected products are supplied for shipment. We see this part of our work as the most important component of long-term cooperation.

Highest, first and second grades. All types, shapes and sizes for the most demanding buyer.
